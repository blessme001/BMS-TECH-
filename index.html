Building tech that works for you

BMS Tech is a one-stop technology solutions provider based in Nigeria. We combine professional web development, hardware consultancy, and hands-on tech support to deliver real value for individuals and businesses.

From powering businesses with custom web applications to solving day-to-day device challenges — we speak your language and deliver results you can see. We also specialize in solar energy solutions, helping clients monitor, size, and manage their off-grid power systems.
